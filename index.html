Billing Rules
① Free Time = CLD + 3 days (4 days incl. CLD)
② General Cargo Wharfrent starts day after free time ends
③ Rate: Tk × ton × day (slab-wise)
④ River Dues = 33 Tk/ton (flat)
⑤ Landing = ≤3t→90 / >3t–≤20t→180 / >20t→250 Tk/ton
⑥ Removal = 7× slab (landing ✓) or 8× slab (landing ✗)
⑦ Hoisting = 125% of landing slab rate · Self Drive = 50% of hoisting rate
⑧ Inside = full wharfrent (inside tons)
⑨ Outside = ½ wharfrent (outside tons)
⑩ Payable basis: total tons (free time) / inside-outside (wharfrent)
⑪ Levy added without VAT
⑫ Landing/Removal/Hoisting auto-computed
⑬ Part Billing: Slab progression never resets — only weight changes per stage
⑭ Self Drive (Hoisting ✓): SD tons billed at Car Billing rates — old/new split applies at 23/07/2024
